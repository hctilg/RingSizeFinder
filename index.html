The length of the string wrapped around your finger is equal to the circumference of the circle.
We know that the circumference of a circle is calculated using the formula C = 2 × π × R.
Therefore, once we know the string's length (the circumference),
we can calculate the ring's inner diameter using the formula D = 2 × R = C ÷ π.
